General

What is the Krow Network?

The Krow Network is a decentralized application that aims to streamline employment onto the internet, using blockchain technology.

What is a blockchain?

A blockchain is essentially a ledger that records every action on the blockchain, and nothing can be overwritten. For the purposes of the Krow Network, that includes hiring, firing, applying, etc.

What powers the Krow Network Blockchain?

The Krow Network uses Hyperledger Composer, an open source system developed by the Linux Foundation.

Why does the Krow Network use a blockchain?

The Krow Network uses a blockchain to ensure speed and reliability, as well as securing all data stored on the network. This allows for a more open and transparent hiring process, where an employer can verify everything on a user's Smart Resume.

What is a "Smart Resume"?

A smart resume is a resume hosted on the Krow Network blockchain. The smart resumes allow for an employer to trust the contents of a resume, because it is all verified by the blockchain. It takes the guessing games out of the hiring process. Since the resume is immutable(can't be changed), employers can trust that the information on the network is true.

Applicant

What is an Applicant?

An applicant is a user on the Krow Network that can apply for jobs, and work for companies

What can an applicant do?

An applicant can apply, remove their application, confirm a hire request, resign, and request to complete jobs.

How do I edit my profile?

Click the edit button on your profile.

How do I apply for a job?

Find the job you want, and click the apply button. You can also remove your application by using the same button.

A company wants to hire me! What do I do?

Go to the job page (look under the hire requests tab under "jobs" in the nav bar) and click the job. Then click the Accept Hire button.

I'm done. How do I quit?

Go to the job page (look under the inprogress jobs tab under "jobs" in the nav bar) and click the job. Then click the Resign button, enter your reason, and click confirm.

I finished my job. How do I tell the employer?

Go to the job page (look under the inprogress jobs tab under "jobs" in the nav bar) and click the job. Then click the Complete Job button. The employer then needs to confirm or deny the completion request.

Employer

What is an Employer?

An employer is a user on the Krow Network that can create jobs, and hire applicants, among other functions.

What can an employer do?

An emplopyer can post jobs, hire, fire, request to hire, deny applicants, etc.

How do I edit my profile?

Click the edit button on your profile.

How do I create a job?

Click the "Post Job" button and fill out the fields.

An applicant applied! What do I do?

Go to the job page (look under the available jobs tab under "jobs" in the nav bar) and click the job. Then you can see applicants. Click the view profile button to look at them, and then click the check or x to request to hire them or deny them. Once you deny them, they can't apply again.

How do I edit a job?

Go to the job page. Then click the edit button.

The applicant has requested to complete their job. What do I do?

Go to the job page. If you think their work is adequate enough, confirm the job. Otherwise, deny it and the applicant can request completion another time.

How do I fire the applicant?

Go to the job page. Under the employee tab, you can click the fire button, and enter a reason, and confirm it.

Other

I have a dispute. What should I do?

Get in contact with us. The Krow Network cannot and will not make a binding, lawfully enforcable decision. All we will do is provide the facts.
